About Me

My names are Isaac Simbeye, a final-year Software Engineering student at the University of Zambia. My background combines academic knowledge in system design, databases, and web development with real-world business experience. I am passionate about building practical systems that solve everyday problems, such as integrating point-of-sale operations with e-commerce platforms. My goal is to leverage technology to modernize small businesses in Zambia, making them more efficient and customer-friendly. What makes me unique is my ability to connect theory with practice — I apply classroom concepts directly to my business, ensuring my projects are both academically rigorous and practically useful.
